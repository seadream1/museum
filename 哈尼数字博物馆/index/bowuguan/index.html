哈尼梯田文化博物馆

Hani Rice Terraces Cultural Museum

哈尼梯田文化博物馆位于民族文化浓郁而美丽的哈尼小镇，距县城南沙45千米，是世界文化景观遗产——红河哈尼梯田农耕文化的集中展示区，是元阳县文化和旅游融合发展的典范。 哈尼梯田文化博物馆于2019年3月31日建成开馆，占地面积4786.14平方米，建筑面积7860平方米。

TThe Hani Rice Terraces Cultural Museum is located in the culturally rich and picturesque Hani Town, 45 kilometers from Nansha County. It serves as a central showcase for the agricultural heritage of the Honghe Hani Rice Terraces, a UNESCO World Cultural Landscape, and stands as a model of integrated cultural and tourism development in Yuanyang County. The museum was officially opened on March 31, 2019, covering a total area of 4,786.14 square meters, with a built-up area of 7,860 square meters.

博物馆位置 Museum Location

哈尼梯田文化博物馆位于云南省红河哈尼族彝族自治州元阳县新街镇哈尼小镇内，距离县城南沙约45公里。

The Hani Rice Terraces Cultural Museum is located in Hani Town, Xinjie Town, Yuanyang County, Honghe Hani and Yi Autonomous Prefecture, Yunnan Province, approximately 45 kilometers from Nansha County.

主要展示物 Main Exhibits

博物馆展示了丰富的哈尼族农耕文化遗存，包括传统农具、民族服饰、生活器具等实物展品。

The museum displays a rich collection of Hani agricultural cultural relics, including traditional farming tools, ethnic costumes, household utensils and other physical exhibits.

特色展品包括：哈尼族传统织布机、梯田耕作工具、祭祀用品、民族乐器等。

Featured exhibits include: traditional Hani looms, terraced farming tools, ritual objects, ethnic musical instruments, etc.
